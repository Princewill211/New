Aims And Scope

The Faculty of Biological Sciences, Alex Ekwueme Federal University Ndufu-Alike, Ebonyi State of Nigeria is the publisher of
International Journal of Biological Science and Molecular Research.It publishes in English, peer reviewed articles in this quarterly multi
disciplinary journal , with the aim of disseminating information on multi-disciplinary research and sundry issues in Biological Sciences.
The scope of the journal covers areas of Biology, Botany, Zoology, Biochemistry, Mycology, Plant Pathology,
Microbiology, Biotechnology, Molecular Biology and Genetics, Agricultural Science, Parasitology, Entomology, Medical Sciences,
Environmental biology and other related fields.
