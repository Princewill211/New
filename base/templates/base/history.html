History Of The Journal

The International Journal of Biological Science and Molecular Research is a publication of the Faculty of Biological Sciences,
Alex Ekwueme University, Ndufu-Alike, Ebonyi State, Nigeria. The faculty houses four programs - Biochemistry, Biology ( Botany
and Zoology), Biotechnology and Microbiology.

This journal is a maiden publication, and double blind review was followed to review all the articles published. Quality
academics were involved in the review of these articles, which are of high quality . The articles seek to disseminate scientific
knowledge which contribute to global knowledge towards food security, good health, environmental, technological, economic
and sustainable development of any nation.

The Editorial team has worked tirelessly to bring this journal into a dream come through, and I thank them immensely.


We invite academics in all areas of agriculture, animal science, food technology and engineering, crop science and production,
medical molecular etc. to submit their articles for publication in this journal.

Professor Onyekachi Francis Akanu Ibiam

Editor-In -Chief
